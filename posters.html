The New Face of DotGNU

The goal of this project is to provide DotGNU with a new logo, slogan, and poster. With the upcoming 0.5.0 release of pnet, and a new year beginning, it is a good time to redress DotGNU and let the world know what we've been working on. This project will compose of several separate sections:

Poster

This new poster will emphasize the freedom aspects of Free software, raise awareness for DotGNU, and briefly describe what DotGNU consists of. We will make sure that these posters are put up in highly-visible places such as university campuses.

Please use GIMP, the GNU Image Manipulation Program for creating graphics files for this project, and put them under the GNU Free Documentation License.

Slogan

While our current slogan of Making The Web Work For You is great, we would like to see what other people can come up with. So if you have some witty verbiage you'd like to spew out, let us know, and you may get to see your words plastered all over the world.

Logo

Same story with the logo; we think the one we have rocks, but I am also excited to see what other people can come up with. So please, if you're talented in this sort of thing, help us out.

 

Distribution and Fund Raising Organizer

We need someone to organize distribution and the funds for poster printing. If you have experience in this area and would like us to be forever indebted to you, please contact us.

These are some of the current ideas we have

  • It should be possible to find at every campus at least volunteer who cares enough about Free Software to help us out by making sure that the posters are put up in good places.
  • However, producing the posters will require funding. We need a significant number of posters to reach all computer science departments (how many are there, worldwide, anyway?). Postage will be a significant expense too.
  • How do we get funding for this? Maybe it is best to approach companies which are not in the software business concerning this.
  • Since this project needs funding anyway, we could try to get some additional funding so that we can hold a competition and have some nice prizes for those who contributed significantly to what is in the end chosen as DotGNU poster.

Contact Us

The coordinating committee for this project currently consists of Ian Fung (irc nick: alias) and Norbert Bollow (irc nick: nb). Both can often be found on the irc channel #dotgnu at irc.freenode.net and they can also be reached via the email address posters@dotgnu.org. Details about contest dates and submissions will be posted later.
