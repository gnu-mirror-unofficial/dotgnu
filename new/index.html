DotGNU:
Making the web work for you!

DotGNU is a project with a clear purpose: to deliver a cross-platform compatible webservices framework using free software utilities to get the job done. Users will have full control of their information and their choice of providers to access webservices. Developers will have unprecedented flexibility, ease of development and control of their creations. Implementors have the capability to inexpensively provide solutions to their target audiences and have significant control over the behavior of their offerings. All of this is enabled by the Free Software product that we call DotGNU.
