DotGNU Portable.NET Worthwhile Links

This page contains links to various websites related to DotGNU Portable.NET.

  • The development of DotGNU Portable.NET is based in Australia, at Rhys Weatherley's company: Southern Storm Software, Pty Ltd. (Because the US legal system is so terribly broken on matters related to software patents, it is important for strategic legal reasons that the Portable.NET development efforts are organized by a company in a country where US patents have no legal force.)
  • The Neil Cawse Portable.net homepage has System.Windows.Forms screenshots, information about the DotGNU Coding competition, and "PNETLIB Visual Studio 1.1 Projects" downloads.
  • wx.NET is a C# wrapper for the wxWindows cross-platform GUI framework. wx.NET developer Bryan Bulten (irc nick: malenfant) is often on the #dotgnu irc channel.
  • Qt# is a C# wrapper for the Qt GUI toolkit, on which the KDE desktop environment for GNU/Linux is based. Qt# developer Marcus Urban (irc nick: marcusU) is often on the #dotgnu irc channel.
