DotGNU Thought and Feature Request List

This page intends to assist in the convergence process of ideas presented on the diverse mailing lists. It tries to keep track of the requested features, their pros and cons and planned version.

A per application FRL will follow.

The table is divided in "Part", "Brief" (a headword), "Description" (description, pros and cons, info and some randomly1 selected posts), "Status" (describing the status of convergence or maybe already released specs) and "V" (the planned version).

1 The page author tries to select the most descriptive posts, though this might not always reflect his own opinion nor that of the list.
2 Commonly used acronyms.
